Healthy Eating

Bill CookJan 28, 2018

Eating a healthy, balanced diet is an important part of maintaining good health, and can help you feel your best. This means eating a wide variety of foods in the right proportions, and consuming the right amount of food and drink to achieve and maintain a healthy body weight. Eating healthily is good for both your mental and physical health!


If you keep only good food in your fridge, you will only eat good food.

Erric McAdams

Make it easy on yourself..

The best way to start eating more healthily is to find recipes you love with food that is good for you. A recipe I would recommend is spicy watermelon tomato salad. Adding chilis gives it some brilliant flavour too!

Key Points

  • Hard to start, but worth it
  • Positive impact on your health
  • Find recipes you love
