FAQs

Why choose us?

We are a modern yet simple way to watch your favourite shows online, and at a fair price too. Our users are always very satisfied with their experience upon joining us.

How much will I be paying?

Our service only costs £5 per month, though we run half price discounts during Easter and Christmas! You won't find a better price for a service like ours anywhere else.

How can I contact you?

You can use the 'Contact Us' button in the navigation bar or the bottom of the page in order to send us a message. We do not save your information for any other purpose.

Can I cancel my subscription?

If you are unsatifisfied with our service, you can cancel your subscription within a week and receive a refund. Otherwise, you can cancel and you will not be billed when next payment cycle arrives.
